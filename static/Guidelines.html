Guidelines for chat users

  1. Use only a single phrase per line
  2. Do not use contractions
  3. Do not use punctuation except for ?
  4. To write an answer, such as: Yes, I can
    Write it as two seperate lines.
  5. Words score +1 if good, -1 if [bad]

Limitations of this app

  1. Correct words belong to a list of about 8000 most common words.
    Words will be considered incorrect if not on this list.
  2. Except for most country names, there are no proper nouns on the list.
  3. Analysis of structure is limited to verb position analysis.
  4. Subject verb agreement is analysed only for pronouns and be/have.
  5. App cannot distinguish noun from verb for the same spelling
    e.g. Can I have a can of Coke?

Future Improvements

  1. Simple Improvements:
    Add contractions and more proper nouns to the word list.
  2. Incremental Improvements requiring additional programming:
    Complete analysis of verb structure
    Allowing two clauses per message.
    Plural noun check
    Allowing more punctuation
